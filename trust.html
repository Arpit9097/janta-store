About SSHD Trust

Committed to Social Welfare and Community Development

SSHD Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the community through various social welfare initiatives. Founded in 2014, we have been working tirelessly to improve the lives of people in Tajpur and surrounding areas.

Our mission is to create sustainable change through education, healthcare, women empowerment, and community development programs. We believe in the power of collective action and community participation to bring about positive transformation.

Our Mission

To empower communities through sustainable development initiatives, focusing on education, healthcare, women empowerment, and social welfare. We strive to create lasting positive change by addressing the root causes of social issues and building capacity within communities.

  • Provide quality education to underprivileged children
  • Improve healthcare access in rural areas
  • Empower women through skill development
  • Support community infrastructure development

Our Vision

To create a society where every individual has access to basic necessities, opportunities for growth, and the ability to lead a dignified life. We envision communities that are self-reliant, inclusive, and sustainable.

  • Eliminate poverty and inequality
  • Ensure quality education for all
  • Create healthy and sustainable communities
  • Foster social harmony and unity
